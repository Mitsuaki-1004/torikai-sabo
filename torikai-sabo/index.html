About

閑かな空間で
一煎のお茶を One Cup of Tea
in a Quiet Space

福岡市城南区鳥飼。閑静で穏やかな住宅街にたたずむ日本茶喫茶です。

Torikai Sabo is a Japanese tea house nestled in the quiet residential streets of Torikai, Jōnan-ku, Fukuoka.

丁寧に一煎一煎いれたお茶と、静かな時間の流れをお届けします。綺麗な水色は、良いお茶の証明。そのひと口に、心の閑りを感じてください。

Each cup is brewed with care and intention — one serve at a time. The clear, beautiful color of the tea is a mark of its quality. With every sip, we invite you to find stillness.

電話のない喫茶。それは、この場所を訪れるすべての方に、本当の静けさを贈るためです。

We have no phone — a deliberate choice, so that every guest can step into true quietude from the moment they arrive.

Concept

鳥飼茶房の
三つのこだわり Our Three
Commitments

01

一

丁寧な一煎One Cup at a Time

茶葉の個性を引き出すため、一煎一煎を大切に。綺麗な水色は良いお茶の証明です。 Each cup is brewed individually to bring out the unique character of the tea. A clear, bright color is the mark of quality.

02

静

閑かな空間A Space of Stillness

電話のない喫茶。心に閑りをもたらす静かな環境で、ゆったりとした時間をお届けします。 No phone. No rush. Just a quiet, unhurried space designed to bring calm to your heart.

03

縁

地域とのつながりRooted in Community

福岡・城南区鳥飼の穏やかな町に根ざし、地域の方々とともに育まれてきた茶房です。 We are proud to be rooted in the gentle neighborhood of Torikai, growing together with the local community.
